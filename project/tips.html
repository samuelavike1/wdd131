Core Strategies

Time Blocking

Plan your day in focused blocks (50–90 minutes) with short breaks. Reserve harder tasks for your peak energy time.

Active Recall

Regularly quiz yourself without notes. Turn headings into questions; answer out loud before checking.

Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks) to strengthen memory.

Distraction Management

Silence notifications, use a timer, and study in a consistent place. Keep only what you need on the desk.
